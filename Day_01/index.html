Hi I'm Chris

I'm currently studying UI design at The Iron Yard. Art has always been my greatest passion because of the sheer freedom it brings, and how it's not generalized to one form.


I grew up In New Braunfels, TX where I spent most of my time skateboarding, wake boarding, and writing music. The style and culture of these activities has played a big role I my life, some which will shape my designs in the future. I now reside in San Marcos, TX with my wife and five month old daughter. In our free time we like to hike, and try interesting fruit like durian. I tend to stick with bands and not try new ones all the time. You will usually hear me jamming Manchester Orchestra, and now Emancipator for task motivation.
